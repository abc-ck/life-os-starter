# prompt 5 — first-week review

**Bottom line:** Calibrates the system from actual use instead of restarting or adding speculative complexity.

**When to read this:** Use after roughly one week of real capture and agent-assisted work.

```text
Review my first week using this vault.

Read the current maps, inbox, project/area indexes, journal records, and any
feedback I provide. Then identify:
- what I actually used
- what I avoided or forgot
- where retrieval or filing felt confusing
- which draft skill proved useful
- which structure is unused or premature
- what one improvement would remove the most friction

Do not redesign the whole system. Recommend at most three changes, explain the
tradeoffs in plain language, and wait for approval before editing. After any
approved changes, update maps/metadata, validate, and commit according to my
repository rules.
```
