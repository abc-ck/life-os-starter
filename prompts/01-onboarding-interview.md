# prompt 1 — onboarding interview

**Bottom line:** Starts a one-section-at-a-time interview without editing the vault.

**When to read this:** Give this prompt to Claude or Codex at the beginning of setup.

```text
We are personalizing this Obsidian agent-system starter for me.

Read these files first:
- AGENTS.md
- START-HERE.md
- os/me.md
- os/agent-rules.md
- os/vault-map.md
- os/retrieval.md
- os/onboarding-interview.md

Then conduct the onboarding interview exactly as described in
os/onboarding-interview.md.

Important rules:
- Ask one section at a time.
- Let me answer naturally and ask useful follow-ups.
- I may skip anything sensitive.
- Do not edit or create files during the interview.
- Do not copy another person's folders, priorities, routines, or identity.
- Prefer the smallest useful system.
- At the end, present a proposed blueprint and exact change list.
- Wait for my explicit approval before building.

Start with a short explanation of the process in plain language, then ask
Section 1 only.
```
