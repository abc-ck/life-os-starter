# research synthesis

## purpose

Turn source-heavy research into a decision-ready brief with evidence, caveats, and a next action.

## trigger

Market scans, comparisons, current information, source intelligence, or research questions.

## steps

1. Define the decision the research supports.
2. Check existing vault knowledge before researching again.
3. Prefer primary and current sources.
4. Separate fact, assumption, and interpretation.
5. State conclusion, strongest evidence, strongest caveat, and next action.
6. Save only durable findings in the appropriate home.

## boundaries

- Do not overstate weak evidence.
- Verify legal, financial, medical, security, and time-sensitive claims.
- Do not bury the answer in source dumps.
