# weekly review

## purpose

Show what moved, what drifted, what should continue, and what should stop consuming attention.

## trigger

Weekly reset or when the owner feels scattered.

## steps

1. Summarize actual work and decisions, not intentions.
2. Identify drift, stuck loops, and repeated friction.
3. Review inbox, projects, areas, and commitments.
4. Choose the owner-approved number of next priorities.
5. Recommend at most one system improvement when evidence justifies it.

## boundaries

- Keep it concise and practical.
- Do not redesign the system weekly.
- Do not activate parked ideas without owner approval.
